Samora Moisés Machel

First President of Mozambique, Samora Moisés Machel.

  • 1993 - Born in the village of Chilembene, in the Gaza Province of Mozambique.
  • 1954 - Leaves his family's Province to start studying in the capital city of Maputo to become a nurse.
  • 1962 - He gave up nursing and volunteered for the Mozambique Liberation Front (FRELIMO), which was an organization dedicated to the liberation of Mozambique from colonial rule.
  • 1963 - He received military training and the following year he led FRELIMO's first guerilla attack against Portugal in northen Mozambique.
  • 1969 - He had become Commander-in-Chief of FRELIMO.
  • 1970 - He was elected as president of FRELIMO.
  • 1975 - On the 25th June 1975, Mozambique was finally granted its freedom and Machel became Mozambique's first president.
  • 1986 - He died in a plane crash on the 19th October, at the age of 53.
